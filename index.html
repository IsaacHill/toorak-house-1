Location

New York style home situated 100m from the heart of Toorak village in Melbourne.

Ross St Toorak is an 3 storey, 3 bedroom fully furnished New York style home situated 100m from the heart of Toorak village in Melbourne. It is also a short walk to Hawksburn village, 3min drive to High st (Armadale) shopping 10min drive to Crown Casino and Melbourne City centre making it a great central location to access all the dining, cafes, shopping and sporting events Melbourne has to offer. Indeed some the cities' best restaurants and boutique fashion, furniture, bridal and antique shopping are within a 5-10 minute drive. There is a tram stop just 100m away on Toorak Rd. It is a 3-5min drive to Monash Freeway.

Furnishings

outfitted to a high luxury standard with lovely artwork

The home is finished and outfitted to a high luxury standard with lovely artwork, furnishings and bedding of the highest standard. It has a fully appointed kitchen and separate laundry with washer/ dryer/ sink. There is a combined large dining-lounge space (incl 60in TV) and outdoor private back courtyard. All bedrooms are of a generous size, fully carpeted with an ensuite in the very large master bedroom and second bathroom servicing the other two rooms. The house has full climate control (heating/ cooling throughout). A sitting room at the entrance is a quiet space with open fireplace for reading and relaxing. On the ground/ entrance floor is a third toilet. There is private lower level lock up garage parking for 2 vehicles.
